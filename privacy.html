Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ScienceStar ("we", "our", "us") operates the website primayscience.org. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. Please read it carefully.

1. About This Site

ScienceStar is a free educational platform providing Singapore Primary Science revision materials for students in Primary 3 through PSLE (ages 9–12), their parents, and teachers. The site offers quizzes, flashcards, study notes, and diagrams aligned to the MOE 2026 Science syllabus.

2. Information We Collect

ScienceStar is designed to operate without collecting personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require any registration, login, or account creation.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Quiz scores, XP points, and progress data are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not operate any servers that store user data.

3. Children's Privacy

ScienceStar is used primarily by children aged 9–12, together with their parents and teachers. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with applicable child privacy laws including Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.
  • We do not allow children to create accounts or submit personal information.
  • All advertising served on this site is configured as non-personalised, meaning ads are not targeted based on individual user behaviour or personal data.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has inadvertently provided personal data to us, please contact us at hello@primayscience.org and we will take immediate steps to address the matter.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising

ScienceStar displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. These advertisements help us keep the site free for all students. Important details about our advertising:

  • All ads are served as non-personalised ads (NPA). This means Google does not use your browsing history or personal profile to select ads.
  • Google AdSense may use cookies solely for the purposes of ad frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and fraud and abuse detection.
  • No behavioural profiling, interest-based targeting, or remarketing is applied to users of this site.
  • Google's advertising practices are governed by the Google Advertising Policy and the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

ScienceStar itself does not set any cookies. However, Google AdSense may place cookies on your device for the limited purposes described above. You have the following options regarding cookies:

  • You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • Browsing in your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent cookies from being saved after your session ends.
  • You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

ScienceStar uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts — Used to load web fonts. Google may log font requests. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — Used to display advertisements. See above and the Google Advertising Policy.

We do not use any analytics services (such as Google Analytics) and do not track user behaviour on this site.

7. Data Retention

Since ScienceStar does not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Any quiz progress data stored in your browser's localStorage can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage.

8. Your Rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you (we hold none).
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal data.

Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are satisfied by our design. If you have any concerns, please contact us.

9. Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we take reasonable precautions to ensure the security of our website. The site is served over HTTPS and we do not operate databases containing user information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ScienceStar after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

ScienceStar is designed specifically for Singapore primary students aged 9 to 12. We do not host user-generated content, discussion forums, or social features. All AdSense advertising is configured to serve non-personalised ads only — ads are shown based on page content, not individual user profiles. We do not sell user data to any third party. Quiz and flashcard progress stored in your browser's localStorage is held locally on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Singapore and the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). For users accessing ScienceStar from outside Singapore, your information may be processed in Singapore and in countries where our third-party providers operate, including the United States where Google's servers are located.

12. Plain English Summary

ScienceStar does not collect your name, email, or personal details unless you choose to email us. Your quiz progress is saved in your own browser — we cannot see it. We show ads to keep the site free, but those ads are not personalised for children. We use anonymised Google Analytics data to understand how many people visit. We will never sell your information. Any questions — email us and we will answer directly.

A note on advertising and children's privacy

ScienceStar shows Google AdSense advertisements to cover the costs of running the site. We want to be transparent about what this means in practice, particularly for families with young children using the site.

All advertising on ScienceStar is configured as non-personalised. This means that Google does not use any individual browsing history, cookies, or profile data to select which ads to show on ScienceStar. Ads are selected based on the content of the page — a page about photosynthesis might show ads relevant to education or science — but not based on who is reading it. This configuration applies to all visitors to ScienceStar and does not require users to opt out individually.

Google's own policies prohibit personalised advertising on pages where the primary audience is children under 13. Since ScienceStar serves primary school students who may be 9 to 12 years old, we have applied the non-personalised ads setting across the entire site rather than attempting to distinguish between child and adult visitors on individual pages.

We have no control over the specific ads that appear on ScienceStar beyond this configuration choice. If you see an ad that appears inappropriate for the context or audience, please email us with a description and we will investigate whether any additional controls can be applied. We take the appropriateness of content on ScienceStar seriously, including the advertising that appears alongside our educational material.
